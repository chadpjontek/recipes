Ingredients:
 - 2 teaspoon white vinegar
 - 4 teaspoons pickled jalapeño juice
 - 4 teaspoons chopped pickled jalapeños
 - 1/3 cup heavy whipping cream
 - 4 slices of white american chesse
 - 2 slices of pepper jack chesse

Directions:
  1. In a small sauce pan, add all ingredients
     and heat to med-low for approximately 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  2. Continue to heat and stir until desired thickness obtained.
